## Portfolio Case Study

### Problem

An e-commerce marketplace needs to understand revenue growth, customer behavior, seller quality, delivery performance, and retention opportunities.

### Approach

I built a reproducible analytics workflow from raw Olist CSV files to cleaned analytical outputs. The pipeline standardizes data, preserves relational keys, builds business metrics, and exports tables for BI. SQL queries support interview-style analysis and KPI validation. RFM and cohort models translate customer behavior into retention strategy.

### Impact

The final project gives stakeholders a practical view of sales performance, logistics bottlenecks, seller quality, product opportunities, customer segments, and revenue growth actions.
